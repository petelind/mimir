Quick Tips
  • Workflows are optional: You can add them now or later.
  • Good workflow names: "Discovery Phase", "User Research", "Design Sprint"
  • Description: Explain what activities this workflow covers.
  • Multiple workflows: Break complex processes into logical steps.
